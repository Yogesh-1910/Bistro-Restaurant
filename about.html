Our Expertise

Chef Gabrielle Leclair - French Cuisine Maestro:

With over two decades of culinary experience honed in the heart of France, Chef Gabrielle Leclair brings a mastery of French cuisine to the kitchen of Bistro. Trained under Michelin-starred chefs in Paris.

Chef Alejandro Martinez - Latin Fusion:

A native of Latin America with a flair for culinary innovation, Chef Alejandro Martinez infuses the flavors of his heritage with a modern twist at Bistro. Drawing inspiration from his travels across the region.

Chef Mei Ling - Asian Cuisine Virtuoso:

Hailing from the bustling streets of Hong Kong, Chef Mei Ling brings a wealth of experience in Asian cuisine to the culinary team at Bistro. Trained in the art of dim sum and noodle making from a young age, Chef Ling's expertise extends across a diverse range of Asian culinary traditions.

Chef Marco Rossi - Italian Culinary Maestro:

Raised in the rolling hills of Tuscany, Chef Marco Rossi brings the rustic charm and rich flavors of Italian cuisine to the table at Bistro. With a background rooted in traditional Italian cooking techniques passed down through generations.
